    • Description

    In this project, I analyzed location data of lightning to investigate energy transfer from earth to space. The goal was to better understand how well the model calculations work mainly through analysis of ground-based lightning detection data, spacecraft LGW measurements, and using ray tracing analysis. After analyzing the ground detection data, my results were compared with previous researchers analysis of spacecraft data to detect patterns in lightning occurence. The main language I used was Python and various Python libraries such as NumPy, Basemap, Scipy, and Matplotlib. I also dabbled in ray tracing, a method to calculate path of waves through a region, on the Summit supercomputer to estimate wave path and the amount of lightning that makes it to space from earth.
